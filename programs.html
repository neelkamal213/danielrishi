Good to know before you enquire

  • Nutritional supplements are assessed individually and typically cost ₹16,000–24,000 every 31 days, depending on the patient's condition — this is separate from the subscription fee above.
  • Acceptance into either program depends on an initial health evaluation, including liver function and haematology reports, and is not automatic.
  • A caretaker must be a blood relative of the patient and available in person, 24/7, for the duration of the subscription.
  • For complications such as ascites, pleural effusion, dialysis needs, very high sugar levels, minor surgery, biopsy, or PET/CT/MRI scans, patients should continue to work directly with their hospital or treating physician.

This program is a coaching and wellness support service. It is designed to work alongside — not replace — care from your treating oncologist or physician. Please continue regular consultation with your medical team throughout.
